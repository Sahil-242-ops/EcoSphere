# 🌍 EcoSphere – AI-Powered Carbon Footprint Intelligence Platform

> Helping individuals **Understand ➜ Track ➜ Reduce** their carbon footprint through AI-powered insights and Google Cloud technologies.

---

## 🎯 Challenge Statement

Design a solution that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through simple actions and personalized insights.

---

# 💡 Problem Statement

Climate change is one of the biggest challenges facing our world today, yet most individuals struggle to understand how their daily activities contribute to carbon emissions.

Existing carbon calculators often provide generic estimates without actionable guidance, making it difficult for users to take meaningful steps toward sustainability.

---

# 🚀 Our Solution

EcoSphere is an AI-powered sustainability platform that enables users to:

✅ Understand their environmental impact through real-time carbon footprint calculations.

✅ Track consumption patterns across electricity, water, waste, and transportation.

✅ Reduce emissions using personalized sustainability recommendations generated by Google Gemini.

By combining cloud-native analytics, AI-powered coaching, and intuitive visualizations, EcoSphere transforms sustainability awareness into measurable action.

# 🧠 Prompt Engineering Strategy

To ensure reliable AI recommendations, EcoSphere implements:

### Structured Output Design

Gemini is instructed to generate structured and predictable responses suitable for application processing.

### Context-Aware Personalization

Recommendations are generated using:

- Water consumption
- Electricity usage
- Waste generation
- Transportation behavior

### Validation Layer

All AI responses are validated before presentation to users.

### Fallback Recommendation Engine

If AI services become unavailable, EcoSphere automatically generates deterministic sustainability recommendations.
